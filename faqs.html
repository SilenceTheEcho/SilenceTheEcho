FAQS:

Q: After my request is submitted, how long will it take to generate a Map of Influence?
A: Your request should be complete within 24 hours.

Q: How will I know when my request is complete?
A: You will receive a notification via email or SMS text with a link to your map.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Silence The Echo does not permanently store the data gathered from your social media, it retains the data for the purposes of generating a map and will be deleted within 24 hours. after you access your map or within 48 hours from when you receive notification your map is completed.

Q: What happens if I do not access my map within 48 hours?
A: You will need to resubmit your request as your map and all data pertaining to it will be removed from our database.

Q: What if I dispute the score or rating of a media content provider?
A: The information page for each provider will have an option to file a dispute regarding the score or rating.
